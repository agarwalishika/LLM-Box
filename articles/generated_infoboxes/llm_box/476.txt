The Cholera vaccine is a vaccine used to prevent cholera infection. It is administered orally. The vaccine is marketed under the tradename Vaxchora. It is approved by the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for adults aged 18 64 who are traveling to an area of active cholera transmission. The vaccine offers limited protection to young children.
