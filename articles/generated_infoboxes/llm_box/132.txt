| Details   Julana is a deity in the mythology of the Jumu and Luritja people of Western Australia and the Northern Territory in Australia. He is depicted as an echerous spirit who surprises women by burrowing beneath the sand, leaping out, and raping them. According to the myth, Julana wandered the Earth during the Dreamtime alongside his father, Njirana.
